About Me

Paulina Oshafi is an aspiring developer currently completing Coding Boot Camp at Harvard Extension School provided by Trilogy Education Servicess.

Upon completion of the programming course she will be well poised to take on a role of a front-end, back-end, or even full-stack developer! Among others, she will master HTML, CSS, Java, JavaScript, and gain good understanding of user-friendly website layout and UI principles.

Paulina has background in accounting and finance. In her current role of senior tax transformation and technology analyst, she is helping clients gain efficiency through implementation of various software.
